Pro Quanta, Per Quantum:
For Quantum, By Quantum

Quantum technologies, such as quantum computing, networking, and sensing, are generally best at solving problems that are actually quantum in nature. These not only include problems for which quantum information theory was developed in the first place, such as photon scattering and electron shell interactions, but also problems whose complexities are those in which quantum information science approaches could provide meaningful algorithmic advantages, such as topological data analysis.
